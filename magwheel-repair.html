Workflow design

How the workflow will operate

The proposal below combines a shared call intake flow with separate downstream workflows for Magwheel Repair and Eastern Vehicle Service.

Shared intake flow

1

Customer calls common number

The client calls a common phone number serving both Magwheel Repair and Eastern Vehicle Service.

2

Call forwards to Quikwit

The call is forwarded to Quikwit’s voice AI receptionist without any perceptible delay for the caller.

3

Language + business intent detected

The receptionist establishes the caller’s preferred language and whether the customer needs Magwheel Repair or Eastern Vehicle Service.

Magwheel Repair workflow

Quote-first or booking-first handling for wheel repair enquiries

4.1

AI receptionist understands the issue

The receptionist assesses the wheel-related issue and determines whether the caller can be moved directly into appointment booking or whether a quote must be prepared first.

4.1A

If booking can be done immediately

The AI receptionist presents available appointment slots, books the appointment, and sends the customer a confirmation via WhatsApp.

4.1B

If a quote is required first

The receptionist gathers as much information as possible about the wheel repair need. If photos are required for an accurate quote, the call ends after informing the customer that they will receive a WhatsApp message from Magwheel that they can reply to with wheel photographs. Photos can also be sent by email if needed.

4.2

Business owner is alerted

Once the customer sends photographs via WhatsApp or email, Nitin is alerted that a customer is waiting for a quotation. He can then review the case and close the loop by providing the quote.

4.3

Quikwit follows up after quote sharing

Once a quotation has been provided, Quikwit chatbot continues the conversation, clarifies customer queries, and follows up until an appointment is booked or the customer chooses not to proceed.

4.4

Reminder and post-service messaging

Quikwit chatbot sends appointment reminders and can share the invoice or receipt via WhatsApp after service completion.

Eastern Vehicle Service workflow

Service booking and quote capture for workshop enquiries

5.1

AI receptionist understands service need

The receptionist identifies the type of service needed and determines whether it can directly proceed into appointment booking or whether a quotation should be generated first.

5.1A

If booking can be done immediately

The AI receptionist presents available slots and books the appointment accordingly.

5.1B

If a quote is required first

The AI receptionist gathers the required details to support quote preparation, such as the car make and model, kilometres driven, last service timing, and any other relevant information based on the requested service. The call ends after informing the customer that they will receive a quote via WhatsApp.

5.2

Business owner is alerted

Nitin is alerted that a customer is waiting for a quotation, along with a link to view the captured service details. He can then review and provide the quote.

5.3

Quikwit follows up after quote sharing

Once a quotation has been provided, Quikwit chatbot continues the conversation, addresses queries, and follows up until an appointment is booked or the customer chooses not to continue.

5.4

Reminder and post-service messaging

Quikwit chatbot sends appointment reminders and can share the invoice or receipt via WhatsApp after service completion.
